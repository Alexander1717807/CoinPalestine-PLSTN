About CoinPalestine

CoinPalestine ($PLSTN) is a decentralized digital token built on Polygon (PoS). The project combines community governance, transparent on-chain tokenomics, and a focus on social impact and economic empowerment.

This website contains official links, whitepaper, and resources for developers, exchanges and community members.
